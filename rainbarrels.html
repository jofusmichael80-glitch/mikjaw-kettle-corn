Rain Barrels

Handmade. Practical. Good for your garden and the planet.

What We Make

We handcraft rain barrels right here in Wichita. A rain barrel collects runoff from your roof and downspouts, giving you free water for your garden, lawn, or landscaping — reducing your water bill and helping keep stormwater out of storm drains.

Each barrel is made with care from quality materials and built to last. We're a small operation, so availability varies — check back or reach out to see what's currently in stock.
